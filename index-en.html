Why is it worth learning a new language?

Travels

When you travel, you will be able to communicate efficiently with local people. You will find it easier to deal with problems in a foreign place.

Job

Knowledge of a foreign language allows you to find a well-paid job. This is often a prerequisite when working for international companies.

Culture

When learning a new language, we learn not only the vocabulary or grammar, but also the culture and customs of a given country.

Hobby

It is also a good solution for people who do not like to waste their free time, prefer to develop rather than stand still.
